Context Bridge

Never waste 10 minutes re-explaining yourself to AI again.

The Problem

Every time you start a new conversation with Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI assistant:

  • You re-explain your project context
  • You re-state your preferences
  • You re-describe your role and goals
  • You waste 10+ minutes before getting value

It's exhausting. And it doesn't have to be this way.

The Solution

Context Bridge creates a persistent context file that follows you everywhere.

  1. Create once: Write your context (who you are, what you're building, your preferences)
  2. Host automatically: It's saved as a GitHub Gist (you own it, not us)
  3. Inject everywhere: One-click browser extension loads your context into any AI chat

From 10 minutes of setup ‚Üí 2 seconds of "I'm ready to work."
